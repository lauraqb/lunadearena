About us

Luna de arena is made up by a small group of friends who own four seaside apartments in the Minorcan northern coast, a charming riad in the historical center of Marrakech and three apartments in a town at the side of Sierra Nevada.

The apartaments in Menorca are ideal for family holidays.

The riad in Marrakech is for discerning travelers with a taste for the exotic.

The charming apartments close to Sierra Nevada are ideal for those who want to enjoy nature, quietness and skiing.

Our motto is good quality turism for different budgets and interests.
