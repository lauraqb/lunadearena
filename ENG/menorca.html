General information

Location and description: Arenal d'en Castell

Arenal d'en Castell belongs to the Es Mercadal municipality. It is located on the Minorcan northern coast, on a wonderful shell-shape bay with crystalline waters and surrounded by beautiful landscapes.

The resort offers several services and entertainments.


Services and facilities

In the vicinity (at walking distances), you can find two supermarkets, a pharmacy and a variety of amenities and entertaiments such us restaurants, lively bars with on-site activities, car-rental, boat-rental, among others.

The calm and shallow crystal waters of the bay make them ideal for families with children, but the resort also offers a good variety of water sports.
