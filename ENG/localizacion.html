Arenal d'en Castell

Arenal d'en Castell is located in the Minorcan northern coast, at 12 miles of the capital Mahon and the airport. It belongs to the municipality of Mercadal (2600 inhabitants), where is the highest point of the island: Mount Toro (355m). The area has beaches, coves and landscapes of spectacular beauty.



HOW TO ARRIVE

FROM CIUDADELA

Take the road towards Mahón all the way to Mercadal. Then, take the road turning for Fornells. Just before Fornells, turn right at the detour to Arenal d'en Castell. Keep straight (several kms.) until you reach a roundabout where Arenal d'en Castell is signaled to the left. Follow this road until you see a hotel called Aguamarina. Turning to the left is the entrance to the resort. Then follow the map shown in the web.

FROM THE AIRPORT

In the roundabout, go towards Puerto de Mahón and in the following roundabout follow the road to FORNELLS for about 11 km until a new roundabout where you'll see a signal for Arenal D'en Castell to the right. Follow this road until you see a hotel called Aguamarina. Turning left you'll find the entrance to the resort. Then follow the map shown in the web.

FROM MAHON PORT

Go to the right and in the first roundabout follow the way to Fornells for about 11km until you reach another roundabout where you'll see a signal for Arenal D'en Castell to the right. Follow this road and soon you will see a hotel called Aguamarina. Turning left you'll find the entrance to the resort. Then follow the map shown in the web.
